Dal Recipe

In this page you will learn how to make Dal

Ingredients for Dal

  1. Toor Dal- 1cup
  2. Tomato - 1
  3. Garlic- 2-3 cloves
  4. Any kind of leafy vegetables(Spinach.Fenugreek leaves)
  5. Onion - 1/2
  6. cumin - 1/2tbs
  7. Turmeric powder- 1/2 tbs
  8. Green chillies - 10-15
  9. Oil - 1tbs
  10. Tamraind - 10gms

Ingredients for tempering

  1. Mustard seeds,cumin - 1ts
  2. Onion - 1/2
  3. Garlic - 3-4 cloves
  4. Curry leaves -1 spring
  5. oil - 3ts

Preparation

  1. To make dal rinse all the ingredients with water in a pressure cooker and let it sit unil 3-4 whisltes. Once the pressure is released, smash the dal with the smasher
  2. For Tempering - take small kadai , heat oil add all the ingredients and fry for 1-2mins.Add this to the dal and serve it with rice/chapati
