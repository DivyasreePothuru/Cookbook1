Idly Recipe

In this page you will learn how to make Idly

Ingredients

  1. Urad dal - 1cup
  2. Sooji - 2cups
  3. Baking Soda - Pinch
  4. Salt - As per taste

Preparation

  1. First rinse all the ingredients with water and soak urdal dal and Sooji in water for atleat 8hours. After 8hourse rinse again and blend them nicely by adding little water and make fine batter. Let it rest whole night.
  2. Next morning add pinch salt and pinch baking soda to the batter. Take Idly cooker add 1/4cup water to the cooker and bit tamarind.Apply oil to the Idly plates and add the batter to all the plates .
  3. Let it cook for 15mins and remove all from the plates and serve it hot with peanut chutney
