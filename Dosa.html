Dosa Recipe

In this page you will learn how to make dosa

Ingredients

  1. Rice - 1cup
  2. Urad dal - 1/2cup
  3. Few methi and chana seeds
  4. Baking Soda - Pinch
  5. Salt - As per taste

Preparation

  1. First rinse all the ingredients with water and soak all in water for atleat 8hours. After 8hourse rinse again and blend them nicely by adding little water. Let it rest whole night.
  2. Next morning add pinch salt and pinch aking soda to the batter. heat a pan spread the batter evenly.
  3. Apply bit oil and let it roast for 10-15secs and serve it hot with peanut chutney
